# VetForce

**Your VA benefits, automated.**

VetForce automates the VA benefits claims process — intake wizard, document OCR, submission tracking, and status alerts. Built for veterans who deserve better.

## Features

*   **Intake Wizard:** Guided process to gather all necessary information for your claim.
*   **Document OCR:** Automatically scan and categorize your medical records and supporting documents.
*   **Submission Tracking:** Monitor the status of your claim in real-time, every step of the way.
*   **Status Alerts:** Receive timely notifications on important updates regarding your claim.
